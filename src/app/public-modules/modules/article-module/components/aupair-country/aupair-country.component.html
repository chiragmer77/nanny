Au pair Bible

Info of Au pair rules and agencies in various countries

This is a cultural exchange program. An Au pair is a young girl or boy who go and live with a family in foreign country. The family provide Au pair with food, accomodation and pocket money. In return Au pair do light household tasks and look after children. Its a good oppurtunity for Au pairs to experience other countries and culture. In general most host families are based in Europe and US as this countries have an Au pair program. Most Au pairs come from countries in Asia, Africa and South America. European Au pairs also Au pair in other European countries. Here are details of countries that have Au pair program and you can compare what is best for you.

  • Free Food and Accomodation
  • Host family pays for health insurance
  • Au pair is expected to be single and have no children
  • There is no Au pair opportunity in Europe if you are above 30 years. There is no Nanny visa in Europe
  • Flight Ticket - Depending on country it can be paid by Host family or by Au pair or shared
  • Always good to have a return flight ticket with adjustable dates
  • Rematch - In most countries its possible to change family if there is issues. In some countries its possible to change family only once
  • In Netherlands, Switzerland and USA only agency can apply for visa. For other countries agency is not mandatory
